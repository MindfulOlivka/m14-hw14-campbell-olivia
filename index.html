About me

I like calling myself a very optimistic person and someone who follows their passion. Moving to this country 10 years ago I couldn't even imagine I would be able to make it. But here I am, having 3 degrees and pursuing my 4th one, owning my own business, buying my 2nd house, traveling the world and enjoying every moment of my life. I know all of it wouldn't have been possible if I stayed in my home country, and just like everyone else didn't even try because of the fear of the unknown. As someone who was always giving their 300% I know what it's like to work hard toward your dreams. Over the years I've learned that everything is possible if you believe in yourself.
